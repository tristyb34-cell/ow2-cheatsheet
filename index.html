Tiers are based on the current Reign of Talon Season 1 meta. At Bronze-Silver, hero mastery matters more than tier placement. A B-tier hero you know well will outperform an S-tier hero you just picked up. These tiers reflect general strength and versatility across maps and team comps.

Dive

"Pack of wolves. Pick one target, everyone jumps, get the kill, get out."

Tank Winston, D.Va, Wrecking Ball
DPS Vendetta, Tracer, Genji, Sombra, Pharah
Sup Kiriko, Juno, Mercy, Lucio
Run it when: Enemy has exposed supports. Open maps with angles.
Don't run it when: Enemy has Brig/Moira peel. Tight corridors.

Brawl

"Rugby scrum. Everyone together, crash in, overwhelm with sustain."

Tank Rein, Zarya, JQ, Orisa
DPS Reaper, Bastion, Mei, Sym, Vendetta
Sup Lucio, Moira, Brig, Ana
Run it when: Tight choke maps (King's Row). Enemy is squishy.
Don't run it when: Open sightlines. Enemy pokes you down before you close the gap.

Poke

"Sniper team. Chip them from range, push when they're half dead."

Tank Sigma, D.Va, Orisa, Domina
DPS Ashe, Hanzo, Soldier, Sojourn, Widow, Bastion
Sup Ana, Zen, Baptiste
Run it when: Long sightlines (Junkertown, Circuit Royal). Enemy runs brawl.
Don't run it when: Enemy dives you. Tight maps. DPS can't hit shots.

Rush

"Brawl's faster cousin. Speed boost in, overwhelm before they can set up."

Tank D.Va, Winston, Zarya, JQ
DPS Vendetta, Reaper, Tracer, Mei
Sup Lucio + Juno, Moira, Brig
Run it when: You have Lucio + Juno for double speed. Enemy is slow or static.
Don't run it when: Enemy has lots of CC (stuns, slows). Long sightline maps.
Key difference from Brawl: Rush uses speed to engage fast. Brawl walks in and sustains. Rush is about the initial hit; brawl is about the grind.

Bunker

"A fortress. Plant yourself on a strong position and make the enemy come to you."

Tank Orisa, Sigma, Ramattra
DPS Bastion, Torbjorn, Soldier, Ashe
Sup Baptiste, Ana, Zen
Run it when: Defending a point. Maps with strong high ground positions. Enemy keeps running into you.
Don't run it when: Enemy runs dive (they bypass your setup). You need to push through open ground.
Key difference from Poke: Poke moves and repositions. Bunker plants and holds one spot. Poke is mobile; bunker is a fortress.

Pocket

"Bodyguard comp. One support dedicates everything to one carry DPS."

Tank D.Va (peels for pocket duo)
DPS Ashe, Pharah, Sojourn, Echo
Sup Mercy (pocket) + Ana/Moira (team heals)
Run it when: Your DPS is popping off and can carry with damage boost. Pharah-Mercy is the classic example.
Don't run it when: Enemy dives the pocket duo. Your DPS isn't converting the boost into kills.
Key detail: This isn't a full comp, it's a strategy within other comps. Mercy pockets Ashe while the rest of the team plays poke or dive around them.

Rock-Paper-Scissors

Dive > Poke > Brawl > Dive

If the enemy switches comp style, you need to switch too.

Bronze-Silver Survival Guide

General Rules

Don't trickle. 3+ dead = fall back and regroup.
Focus fire. Shoot what your team shoots. One kill > spread damage.
Ults start fights. Don't waste them saving a lost fight.
Read the kill feed. 2 teammates dead = stop pushing.

Winston

  • Always save Jump Pack for escape. No exit = don't jump in.
  • Target isolated supports, not the tank. Backline is your job.
  • Primal Rage is a survival tool, not just for boops. It refills your HP.
  • Drop bubble on top of the enemy support to trap them inside with you.

Orisa

  • Fortify is your panic button. About to die? Press it. Enemies won't track the cooldown.
  • Javelin Spin + walk forward = push through any choke your team is stuck on.
  • Terra Surge up close only. Walk in first, then pull. Don't try max range.
  • You don't need your team to coordinate. Just hold space and be a wall.

D.Va

  • Matrix is your #1 ability. Eat ults (Grav, Dynamite, rockets). Protect teammates first.
  • Don't chase kills deep. Your team won't follow. You'll lose mech and feed.
  • Self-Destruct only works in tight spaces or combos (Grav, Shatter, small rooms).
  • Bump enemies off high ground with boosters. Contest every angle.

Vendetta

  • Commit fully or don't go. Soaring Slice in, overhead for 130, Whirlwind out or block.
  • Overhead swing is your bread and butter. 130 damage, pierces barriers, crits on tanks.
  • Warding Stance = 75% damage reduction. Block proactively, not reactively.
  • Build Onslaught stacks (hit enemies = faster attacks + movement). Stay aggressive to stay fast.

Reaper

  • Wraith Form = ESCAPE ONLY. Walk into position. Get kills. Wraith out. Never Wraith in.
  • Shadow Step to flanks BEFORE fights start. The animation is a death sentence mid-fight.
  • Death Blossom from above (drop off a ledge). Low rank players never look up.
  • Tight corridors = your playground. Open maps = consider switching hero.

Ashe

  • HIGH GROUND ALWAYS. On the ground = wrong position. Get up there.
  • ADS every shot. Hip-fire is panic-only. The damage difference is massive.
  • Dynamite mid-air detonate into groups. Even missed shots deal huge burn.
  • Coach Gun is your ONLY escape. Save it for divers (Winston/D.Va).

Bastion

  • STOP SITTING STILL. Assault mode to win the fight, then move to a new spot.
  • Recon mode gun is solid at mid-range. Use it. Assault is a finisher, not your default.
  • Artillery (ult) at the START of fights. Three shots on grouped enemies = fight won.
  • Low rank enemies walk the same path repeatedly. Exploit it, then reposition.

Mercy

  • BLUE BEAM > YELLOW BEAM. Full HP target = damage boost. Always. This is the #1 mistake.
  • Learn super jump. It's the difference between dying constantly and being unkillable.
  • Don't fly to dying allies in bad positions. You'll die too. Let them go.
  • Safe rez or NO rez. Dead Mercy is worse than a dead teammate staying dead.

Juno

  • Hyper Ring BEFORE your team engages. Deploy in front of your tank.
  • Your gun can't headshot. Don't flick for heads, just land consistent body shots.
  • Flying for Torpedoes makes you vulnerable. Only fly when safe.
  • Speed boost alone confuses low rank enemies. Use it aggressively to overwhelm.

Zenyatta

  • Discord Orb > your damage output. Keep it on whoever your team is shooting.
  • Stay FAR behind your tank. Zero mobility = instant death if flanked.
  • Pre-charge volley (right-click) around corners. Release on first enemy you see.
  • Save Transcendence for enemy ults: Blade, Blossom, Visor. Don't waste it casually.

Moira

  • HEAL FIRST. Healing orbs in fights, not damage orbs. That's the #1 DPS Moira mistake.
  • Fade = escape ONLY. Never Fade forward into enemies. You will die every time.
  • Damage beam recharges healing juice. Weave damage in between heals.
  • Coalescence goes through allies AND enemies. Aim through your team into theirs.

Ana

  • Stay FAR back. Range is your superpower. Next to Rein = too close.
  • Anti-nade into groups during fights = instant fight winner. Splash is forgiving.
  • Sleep Dart: defensive first (flankers on you), offensive second.
  • Nano the person IN the fight, not random backliners. Nano-Rein swinging = good. Nano-Widow = bad.

Ultimate combos from your hero pool. Coordinate these with your team for easy team wipes.

Nano + Death Blossom

Ana + Reaper

The classic. Ana Nano-Boosts Reaper, he Shadow Steps or Wraith Forms in and pops Death Blossom. 50% more damage + 50% less damage taken = near-guaranteed team wipe if they're grouped.

Tip: Wait for enemy support cooldowns (Suzu, Immortality) before committing.

Nano + B.O.B.

Ana + Ashe

Nano the robot. Yes, you can Nano B.O.B. He becomes an unkillable turret dealing massive damage. Forces the enemy team to either scatter or die.

Tip: Deploy B.O.B. first so Ana can see him, then she Nanos him. Works even when Ana is far away.

Self-Destruct + Primal Rage

D.Va + Winston

Zone + scatter. D.Va throws her mech, Winston goes Primal and knocks enemies INTO the explosion radius. Even if the bomb doesn't kill, Winston finishes them while they scatter.

Tip: Winston Primal just before the bomb goes off, knock enemies away from cover.

Discord + Bastion Config

Zenyatta + Bastion

Delete button. Discord Orb on the tank while Bastion is in Assault Config. The 25% damage amp on Bastion's already insane DPS melts anything instantly. Not an ult combo, but deadly at all times.

Tip: Discord the tank first, then swap Discord to supports once the tank drops.

Anti-Nade + Coalescence

Ana + Moira

No healing allowed. Ana throws Anti-Nade onto grouped enemies, then Moira beams through them with Coalescence. They can't be healed, and Moira's beam does damage + heals your team simultaneously.

Tip: Best into brawl comps where enemies clump together. Anti first, then Coalescence.

Damage Boost + Configuration

Mercy + Bastion

Turret on steroids. Mercy damage-boosts Bastion in Assault Config. Combined with his already high DPS, this shreds tanks in seconds and melts barriers like butter. Works without ults.

Tip: Stay behind cover. If Bastion gets focused, swap to healing beam, then back to boost.

Trans + Death Blossom

Zenyatta + Reaper (Defensive)

Aggressive Transcendence. Zen pops Trans and runs INTO the enemy with Reaper. Reaper Death Blossoms while Zen heals through all incoming damage. The enemy has to burst through Trans healing to kill Reaper.

Tip: Watch for Ana anti-nade. If enemies have Ana, bait the nade first.

Supercharger + Artillery

Orisa + Bastion

Maximum firepower. Orisa drops Supercharger for team damage boost while Bastion rains Artillery shells. The boosted Artillery shots deal devastating damage across the map.

Tip: Place Supercharger behind cover so enemies can't destroy it quickly.

Which of your heroes work best on different map types. Pick based on the map, not just comfort.

Control Maps

"King of the hill. Fights are fast and reset often."

Tank D.Va (always good), Winston (high ground maps like Busan)
DPS Reaper (tight points), Vendetta (close-quarters maps)
Sup Moira (sustain on point), Juno (speed to contest)
Key maps: Busan, Ilios, Lijiang Tower, Nepal, Oasis, Antarctic Peninsula, Samoa

Escort / Payload

"Push the cart. Long sightlines, multiple checkpoints."

Tank D.Va (peel for team), Orisa (sit on cart with Fortify)
DPS Ashe (long sightlines), Bastion (ride the cart + turret)
Sup Ana (sightlines for heals), Mercy (boost Ashe/Bastion)
Key maps: Dorado, Havana, Junkertown, Circuit Royal, Rialto, Route 66

Hybrid

"Capture first, then escort. Point fights are brawly, then it opens up."

Tank D.Va (flex between phases), Winston (first point dives)
DPS Vendetta (first point brawl), then swap Ashe (escort phase sightlines)
Sup Ana (always valuable), Moira (point capture sustain)
Key maps: King's Row, Blizzard World, Eichenwalde, Hollywood, Midtown, Numbani, Paraiso

Push

"Race for the robot. Long rotations, fight for mid control."

Tank Winston (fast rotations, high ground), D.Va (always safe)
DPS Ashe (long sightlines), Vendetta (tight corridors)
Sup Juno (speed for rotations), Zen (discord from distance)

General Rule of Thumb

Open maps (long sightlines) = Ashe, Bastion, Ana, Zen
Tight maps (close quarters) = Reaper, Vendetta, Moira, D.Va
Vertical maps (high ground) = Winston, D.Va, Ashe, Mercy
When unsure = D.Va + Ashe + Ana (safe default combo)

Quick reference: who on your roster counters what, and what counters you.

D.Va Matchups

You counter:
Pharah (fly up + shoot her down)
Widowmaker (dive her, Matrix her shots)
Bastion (Matrix eats his entire output)
Counters you:
Zarya (bubbles feed from your Matrix shots)
Symmetra (beam ignores Matrix)
Reaper (close range shreds your mech)

Winston Matchups

You counter:
Widowmaker (jump on her, she can't escape)
Hanzo (dive him, he struggles up close)
Zenyatta (he's free food if he's alone)
Counters you:
Reaper (melts you at close range)
Bastion (shreds through your HP pool)
Ana (sleep dart + anti-nade shuts you down)

Vendetta Matchups

You counter:
Supports (burst them before they react)
Widowmaker (close distance, she's helpless)
Bastion (flank and burst from behind)
Counters you:
Reaper (out-trades you close range)
Brigitte (stun + armour shuts down your burst)
Cassidy (Magnetic Grenade + headshot kills you)

Reaper Matchups

You counter:
Winston (melt him at close range)
Reinhardt (walk up and shoot his face)
Roadhog (self-heal through his damage)
Counters you:
Ashe (outranges you completely)
Pharah (can't touch her in the sky)
Ana (sleep dart stops Death Blossom)

Ashe Matchups

You counter:
Pharah (hitscan shuts her down)
Junkrat (outrange his spam easily)
Echo (hitscan vs. flying target)
Counters you:
Tracer (too fast, too close)
Genji (deflects your shots, dives you)
D.Va (Matrix eats everything, dives you)

Bastion Matchups

You counter:
Reinhardt (shred his shield, then him)
Orisa (out-DPS her Fortify)
Roadhog (melt him before he heals)
Counters you:
Genji (deflects your turret fire back)
Sombra (hacks you out of Config, you're helpless)
Tracer (flanks you, Pulse Bomb one-shots)

Ana Matchups

You counter:
Roadhog (anti-nade stops his self-heal = dead hog)
Reinhardt (sleep his charge, anti his team)
Any grouped team (anti-nade is devastating)
Counters you:
Tracer (too fast to hit, blinks past your sleep)
Genji (dives you, deflects your nade)
Winston (jumps on you, bubble blocks your heals)

Zen / Mercy / Moira / Juno

Zen counters: Tanks (Discord melts them). Weak to: Any diver.
Mercy counters: Nothing directly. Weak to: Sombra, Tracer (she can't fight back).
Moira counters: Genji (auto-aim goes through deflect), Tracer (Orbs track her). Weak to: Anyone with range.
Juno counters: Slow comps (speed past them). Weak to: Dive assassins.
